VSING aims to be an international governing body of vocal sports. VSING is currently headquartered in Malaysia and is actively expanding overseas essentially across Asia Region. VSING provides the highest standard of vocal sports facilities to ensure proper talent enhancement and development in their career roadmap.

VSING takes the lead in organizing prized tournaments and leagues to supercharge the vocal sports development, allowing vocal athletes to compete one another, resulting in an exciting matches that draws the public attention.
